A philosophy of practice.

Dr. Shiva Harikrishnan has spent her career at the intersection of surgical precision and patient trust. She trained in obstetrics and gynaecology in India, became one of the early adopters of laparoscopic surgery in the region, and has practised in Dubai for more than two decades — serving as Senior Consultant Obstetrician and laparoscopic surgeon at Medcare Royal Speciality Hospital, Al Qusais.

Her sub-specialty interests are advanced laparoscopy, the medical and surgical management of endometriosis, and the care of high-risk pregnancies — including pregnancies in women with previous surgery, autoimmune disease, or recurrent pregnancy loss. She is the world's first female surgeon to have been accredited as Master Surgeon in Multidisciplinary Endometriosis Care (MSMEC) by the Surgical Review Corporation, USA — and also holds accreditation as Master Surgeon in Minimally Invasive Gynecology (MSMIG).

A note on approach.

Many of the questions women bring to a gynaecologist are not surgical at all. Often the most useful first appointment is a long, unhurried one — with the time to take a real history, the patience to read the previous reports, and the honesty to recommend nothing when nothing is needed.

When surgery is the right answer, it is undertaken with the same rigour: the least invasive approach that will work, performed personally, with continuity of care from consultation to recovery.
