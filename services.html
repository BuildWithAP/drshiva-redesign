Areas of practice

Six programmes. One philosophy.

Every patient is seen, examined, and operated on personally. There is no rotating roster — the surgeon at the consultation is the surgeon in the theatre.

01 / Surgery

Laparoscopic surgery.

Advanced minimally invasive gynaecological surgery is the cornerstone of the practice. Most procedures that historically required open surgery — hysterectomy, myomectomy, ovarian cystectomy, treatment of endometriosis — can now be done laparoscopically, with same-day or next-day discharge and a return to daily life within a week.

The benefits are well-established: smaller incisions, less pain, less blood loss, fewer infections, faster recovery, and almost no visible scarring.

  • Total laparoscopic hysterectomy (TLH)
  • Laparoscopic myomectomy — for fibroids of all sizes and locations
  • Laparoscopic excision of endometriosis (including deep infiltrating)
  • Ovarian cystectomy & oophorectomy
  • Laparoscopic management of ectopic pregnancy
  • Diagnostic laparoscopy & adhesiolysis
02 / Specialist

Endometriosis care.

Endometriosis affects roughly one in ten women, and the average time to diagnosis remains years longer than it should be. A modern endometriosis programme is built around precise diagnosis, complete surgical excision where required, and long-term medical and lifestyle support afterwards.

Dr. Shiva is recognised internationally as a Master Surgeon in Minimally Invasive Gynecology and an Endometriosis Specialist by the Surgical Review Corporation (USA).

  • Comprehensive pain & symptom assessment
  • Imaging-led diagnosis — ultrasound & MRI when indicated
  • Laparoscopic excision (preferred) over ablation
  • Treatment of deep infiltrating endometriosis
  • Fertility-aware surgical planning
  • Long-term follow-up & recurrence prevention
03 / Obstetrics

High-risk pregnancy.

A pregnancy is called “high-risk” when there is something — about the mother, the baby, or the pregnancy itself — that warrants closer monitoring. The list of those somethings is long, but the principle is simple: more frequent reviews, the right tests at the right time, and a delivery plan made well in advance.

Care continuity matters most here. The same consultant from the first scan to the post-natal review.

  • Pre-eclampsia & gestational hypertension
  • Gestational diabetes & thyroid disease in pregnancy
  • Recurrent miscarriage workup & pregnancy support
  • Pregnancy after previous caesarean / surgery
  • Multiple pregnancy management
  • Pregnancy in women with autoimmune or cardiac conditions
04 / Obstetrics

Obstetrics & antenatal.

Routine pregnancy care — from the first positive test through the post-natal weeks — with a calm, considered approach to scans, screening, and delivery planning.

  • Pre-conception counselling
  • Comprehensive antenatal care & trimester scans
  • Normal vaginal delivery
  • Caesarean section (planned & emergency)
  • VBAC — vaginal birth after caesarean
  • Post-natal recovery & lactation support
05 / Fertility

Fertility & conception.

Many fertility journeys start with the wrong investigation, or the wrong order of investigations. A focused fertility consultation begins with the questions: what's the right next test for this couple, what does the imaging actually show, and is there a structural problem that surgery would resolve?

  • Pre-conception health assessment
  • Ovulation induction & cycle tracking
  • Hysteroscopic correction of polyps & intrauterine adhesions
  • Laparoscopic correction of fibroids & tubal disease
  • Recurrent miscarriage investigation
  • Coordination with IVF specialists when indicated
06 / Wellness

Routine gynaecology.

The everyday work of looking after a woman's health — the appointments that prevent the difficult ones later. Annual reviews, contraception, menstrual problems, PCOS, and the menopausal transition.

  • Annual gynaecology examination & cervical screening
  • Contraception counselling & coil fitting
  • Heavy or irregular periods & PCOS
  • Pelvic pain & pelvic floor issues
  • Perimenopause & menopause care
  • HPV vaccination counselling
